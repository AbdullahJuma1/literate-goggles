Who we are

Get to know about our company

HydroAcres targets the problem of extreme water loss as a result of agricultural inefficiency through a low-tech micro controller that can monitor soil conditions and irrigate crops accordingly, controlled from a farmer’s phone. By not requiring complex equipment, a smart phone, or extensive technical knowledge, HydroAcres' product aims to help rural farmers, especially those in Sub-Saharan Africa, irrigate more efficiently—increasing crop yield, and decreasing both financial and environmental costs.

The team also provides supplementary consulting and training on water and agriculture.
